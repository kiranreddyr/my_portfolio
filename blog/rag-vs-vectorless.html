The real insight: Most teams default to vector RAG for everything. But when you chunk a 200-page legal contract into 300-token pieces — you destroy the document map. Vector RAG finds similar text. Vectorless RAG finds the right place. The model is rarely the bottleneck in enterprise AI. Retrieval architecture almost always is.
