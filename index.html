About Me

9 years of shipping products that scale.

I started as a software engineer building IoT apps. What I kept seeing was the same problem: the intelligence existed in the data, but the people who needed it had no way to act on it. That gap between raw enterprise data and decisions on the ground is what I've spent 9 years closing.

At TVS Motors, I own the supply chain platform serving 90+ distributors nationwide on a ₹3,000Cr+ annual revenue base. I've deployed AI replenishment engines, built dealer e-commerce generating ₹50L+ monthly, and defined the architecture for an LLM analytics copilot.
