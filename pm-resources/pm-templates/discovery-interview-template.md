# Discovery Interview Template

- Role / company:
- Segment:
- Date:

## Goal of Interview
- What are we trying to learn?

## Opening
- Thank you for taking the time.
- We are trying to understand your current workflow and pain points.
- There are no right or wrong answers.

## Background Questions
- Can you tell me about your role?
- What does success look like in your job?
- What tools or systems do you use most often?

## Current Workflow
- Walk me through how you currently do this task.
- What triggers the workflow?
- Who else is involved?
- Where does the process slow down?

## Pain Points
- What is the hardest part of this process?
- What causes frustration most often?
- What is manual today that should not be?
- What do you wish you could see earlier?

## Existing Alternatives
- How do you solve this today?
- What workarounds do you use?
- What tools have you tried before?

## Decision and Value
- If this problem were solved well, what would improve first?
- How would you measure whether it helped?
- Who would care most about the outcome?

## Closing
- Is there anything I did not ask that I should understand?
- Would you be open to a follow-up?
