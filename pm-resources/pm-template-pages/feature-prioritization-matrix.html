METHODOLOGY
Scoring Framework Guide

Each dimension is scored 1-5. Define your scale to ensure consistency across evaluations.

User Impact
How many users benefit?
1: Single cohort, 5: Platform-wide
Business Impact
Revenue or retention impact?
1: Minimal, 5: Major ARR mover
Strategic Fit
Aligns with quarterly goals?
1: Tangential, 5: Core pillar
Confidence
How sure are you it will work?
1: Speculative, 5: Proven approach
Urgency
Time-sensitive or blocking?
1: Can wait, 5: Ship this sprint
Effort
Engineering complexity?
1: 1-3 day lift, 5: 4+ week project
