Why this matters

Strategy isn't a 100-page document. It's clarity on what we believe, why we believe it, and how we'll know if we're right.

Quick review

  • Problem clear?
  • Thesis defensible?
  • Bets specific?
  • Metrics measurable?
  • Team aligned?
Context & Background

Where Are We, and Why Now?

Current state & market context
What's happening in the market? What's our position? What triggered this initiative?
Recent learnings & feedback
What have we learned from customers, data, or market trends?
Problem Definition

What Problem Are We Solving?

Core problem statement
A clear, specific statement of the problem. Avoid solutions; focus on the underlying issue.
Who feels this problem acutely?
Segment, user role, company size, use case, etc.
How do we know it's a real problem?
Data points, customer quotes, survey results, cohort analysis, etc.
Customer & User

Who Are We Building For?

Primary user / customer profile
Role, company size, industry, goals, constraints.
Secondary users (if applicable)
Who else will use this? How does their perspective differ?
User's desired outcome
What do they want to achieve? How do they measure success?
Strategic Thesis

Why This Matters to Us

Our thesis (what we believe)
What's the bold statement we're making? If we nail this, what becomes possible?
Strategic fit
How does this align with company vision and direction?
Competitive advantage
Why are we uniquely positioned to solve this? What's our moat?
Goals & Objectives

How Do We Know We Succeeded?

What's the milestone we're targeting?
OKRs (optional but recommended)
Objective 1: [description] | Key Result: [measurable outcome]
